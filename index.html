Project Info Single-family

Dwelling Setup

2026 NEC feeder/service rule: dwelling general lighting and general-use receptacle load uses 2 VA/sq ft. Small-appliance and laundry circuits are still calculated at 1,500 VA per circuit.

Dwelling Loads Per Unit

Dryer minimum is treated as 5,000 VA when a dryer is entered. HVAC uses the larger noncoincident heating or cooling value. EVSE is calculated at 100% of nameplate in this workflow.

Demand Options

For 1–2 dwellings, this calculator uses dwelling-unit demand logic instead of applying the 3+ multifamily optional table. For 3+ multifamily projects, the auto table is included for estimating; the manual override exists so you can match the specific factor your inspector wants.

Common / House Loads

House loads are added separately after the dwelling-unit demand calculation.

Logic Used

• 2026 NEC-style dwelling feeder/service general load: 2 VA/sq ft.
• Small appliance and laundry circuits: 1,500 VA each.
• Optional dwelling method: first 8 kVA at 100%, remainder at 40%, including larger noncoincident HVAC.
• Two-family mode calculates each dwelling unit with dwelling demand instead of 100% connected load.
• 3+ multifamily mode applies the selected multifamily demand factor to the combined dwelling-unit load, including larger HVAC.
• EVSE calculated at entered VA unless separately controlled by an approved load management system.
• Dryer minimum: 5,000 VA when dryer load is used.
